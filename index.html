Our Services

We offer various services. We aim to fulfill your needs and wishes by guiding you through the beautiful city of Venice. Our services are granted by citizens of Venice.

Transportation

We provide transportation throughout Venice. Our Save Venice sailers are local citizens, this means they know the routes by heart.

Food

Eat local food at one of our Save Venice providers. Taste the culture while you hear stories of the families you visit. Homemade pasta is the best pasta!

Accomodation

Sleep in comfortable beds of your choosing!

Information

The locals know best where you should go and how to get there. They are the best source of information about Venice!

About us

Save Venice is created for tourists. We aim to create a better image for Venice, for tourists but also for the locals.

Existing media will draw outsiders into helping restoring Venice. The facilitation of the local people provides a foundation for knowledge exchange. This will give the locals more influence in the restoration of Venice, increasing population growth.

Target group

Working holidy tourists who want to help restoring venice.

Tbe citizens of Venice who will provice accomodation, food and transportation to the working holiday tourists

Vision

Venice is getting a worse image by the year. This is because the city is poorly maintained, buildings are suffering from water damage and citizens are leaving the beautiful city. The city once had 120.000 citizens but it is now reduced to 60.000 citizens.

Mission

Our mission is to create a better environment for tourists and local citizens within Venice.
