Our Services

We offer various services. We aim to fulfill your needs and wishes by guiding you through the beautiful city of Venice. Our services are granted by citizens of Venice.

Transportation

We provide transportation throughout Venice. Our Save Venice sailers are local citizens, this means they know the routes by heart.

Food

Eat local food at one of our Save Venice providers. Taste the culture while you hear stories of the families you visit. Homemade pasta is the best pasta!

Accomodation

Sleep in comfortable beds of your choosing!

Information

The locals know best where you should go and how to get there. They are the best source of information about Venice!
